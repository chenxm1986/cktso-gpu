1. Download CKTSO package from https://github.com/chenxm1986/cktso
2. Copy libcktso.so, libcktso_l.so, cktso.h, and cktso.lic (all from CKTSO package) here
3. Type in "make"
4. Type in "export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=."
4. Type in "./demo add20.mtx" or "./demo_l add20.mtx" or "./demo_c add20.mtx" or "./demo_lc add20.mtx"
